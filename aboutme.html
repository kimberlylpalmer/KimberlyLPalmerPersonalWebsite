About Me

Hello! I'm Kimberly Palmer, a dynamic Web Designer and Full Stack Engineer with a rich background in program and project management, web design, and operations. My career has been a diverse journey, primarily in the healthcare and technology sectors, with meaningful experiences in nonprofit organizations as well. This unique blend of environments has honed my ability to build exceptional teams and create impactful digital solutions.

I proudly hold a degree in Business Administration and Management from Touro University, which laid the foundation for my interdisciplinary approach to technology and design. Further sharpening my technical skills, I am a recent graduate of the Flatiron School's Software Engineering Program, where I mastered the latest in web development and engineering practices.

Beyond my professional life, I find joy and balance in spending quality time with my beloved dog, Thalia, and cat, Lumi. Together with my partner, Mark, we cherish every moment, whether it's in the tranquility of our home or on adventurous outings.

Through my website, I invite you to explore my portfolio, where my projects speak to the passion and dedication I bring to every challenge. Whether you're interested in collaboration or just wish to connect, I'm always open to discussing new opportunities and ideas.
